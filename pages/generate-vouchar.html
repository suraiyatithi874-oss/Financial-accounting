3.1 Debit Voucher Entry

Used for payments and expenses where cash or bank is credited.

  1. Click the Debit Voucher button.
  2. Select Branch: Choose the relevant outlet.
  3. Accounts: Define the Debit account (Expense/Asset) and Credit account (Cash/Bank).
  4. Amount & Narration: Input accuracy-checked values and optional cheque details.
  5. Click "Insert" to add the line to the table.
  6. Click "Save" to finalize and post to the ledger.
×

3.2 Credit Voucher Entry

Used for receiving funds where cash or bank is debited.

  1. Click the Credit Voucher button.
  2. Choose the business unit/branch.
  3. Specify financial value and add a Cheque Reference if applicable.
  4. Review the entry in the Voucher Grid.
  5. Add a Global Narration for an overall summary.
  6. Click "Save" to post.
×

3.3 Journal Voucher Entry

Used for non-cash adjustments, depreciation, or rectifications.

  1. Pick the Branch for the adjustment.
  2. Select multiple ledger accounts for complex multi-line entries.
  3. Hit "Insert" to build the list below.
  4. Ensure total Debits equal total Credits.
  5. Click "Save" to finalize.
×

3.4 Contra Voucher Entry

Used for internal movements (e.g., Cash to Bank or Bank to Cash).

  1. Select the branch where the internal transfer is occurring.
  2. Define the Source and Destination (Cash/Bank).
  3. Type the exact value being moved.
  4. Add a Global Narration (e.g., "Withdrawal for Petty Cash").
  5. Click "Save" to finalize.
×

3.5 Fund Transfer Voucher Entry

Used for moving funds between two different branches.

  1. Select the Sending Branch and Target Branch.
  2. Choose the receiving Cash/Bank account in the target branch.
  3. Enter transfer amount and optional reference info.
  4. Click "Insert" followed by "Save".

3.6 Authorization Workflow

Unauthorized List

Pending vouchers that have been entered but not yet verified.

  • Apply filters (Branch, Type, Date).
  • Tick checkboxes for selection.
  • Click "Authorize" to post to final accounts.

Authorized List

Finalized vouchers that are currently reflected in the ledgers.

  • Search by specific Voucher Code.
  • Select and click "Unauthorize" to pull back for correction.

Maker-Checker Policy

Only users with specific "Checker" permissions can authorize vouchers. Unauthorized vouchers will not appear in financial reports like the Trial Balance or Income Statement.
