Overview

About This System

The Financial Accounting System is designed to record, control, reconcile, and report organizational financial transactions in a secure and auditable manner.

1 Module Purpose

The Financial Accounting System, developed by Tara Tech Ltd, is a comprehensive platform designed to manage organizational finances through the rigorous double-entry accounting method.

By centralizing account structures and automating validation workflows, the system ensures high data integrity, strict internal controls, and seamless generation of statutory financial statements.

2 Main Features

Chart of Accounts

Hierarchical account structure for assets, liabilities, income, and expenses.

Voucher-Based Transactions

Controlled transaction entry using standardized voucher types.

Authorization Workflow

Authorized and unauthorized voucher states to ensure internal controls.

Bank Reconciliation

Matching system records with bank statements to ensure accuracy.

Period Closing

Day End, Month End, and Year End closing for data integrity.

Financial Reporting

Real-time reports for monitoring, compliance, and audits.
