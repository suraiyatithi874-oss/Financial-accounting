7. Reports

Purpose

The Reports menu consolidates key financial statements and ledger records for monitoring, analysis, and audit purposes. It provides branch-level insights and helps verify the accuracy and completeness of transactions across all modules.

Navigation

Menu Path: Main Menu → Reports
